MORE ABOUT US

Founded with a mission to drive sustainable innovation in the energy and automotive sectors, ReFueler is a pioneering startup focused on developing advanced engine-optional solutions for fuel recycling. The company was established in response to the growing global demand for cleaner energy practices and efficient fuel utilization. By combining cutting-edge engineering with eco-conscious design, ReFueler has created a proprietary system that enables fuel recovery and reuse without the need for traditional engine integration.

This novel approach allows for flexible application across various industries - from transportation and logistics to emergency power systems - empowering businesses to reduce fuel waste, lower emissions, and improve operational efficiency. With a team of experts in mechanical engineering, green technology, and sustainable energy systems, ReFueler is committed to redefining how fuel can be recycled and repurposed in a rapidly changing world.

THE PRODUCT

How it works

The Digital ReFueler unit mounts onto the vehicle’s exhaust line, capturing unburned fuel vapors and micro-droplets. These are directed into a separation chamber where the vapors are cooled, condensed back into liquid, and then either returned to the fuel tank or blended with fresh fuel as an additive.

Reliability

In pilot tests on corporate fleets, the system ran continuously for over 1,000 km without any unscheduled maintenance. Its plug-and-play design achieves OEM-level durability, with only routine service required every six months. ​

Compatibility

Engineered for diesel vehicles up to Euro 6 standards, the Digital ReFueler fits both light and heavy-duty engines. Its modular installation accommodates all standard exhaust configurations, enabling quick integration across mixed fleets.

Advantages

Fuel Savings: Up to 24 % reduction in consumption in tests with 50 vehicles, delivering payback within 12 months.

Lower CO₂ Emissions: Actively recovers unburned fuel, cutting greenhouse gas output.

Cost Optimization: Reduces both refueling expenses and routine maintenance through improved engine efficiency.

Safety

All pressure-bearing components comply with European PED directives, and the system maintains standard exhaust temperature and pressure tolerances. Its installation avoids contact with moving parts or high-heat zones to ensure full operational safety.

Scalability

Featuring an expansion-vessel modular design, capacity can be increased simply by adding secondary units. The same cloud-based monitoring platform supports small fleets (2–5 vehicles) up to large operations (>100 vehicles) without additional infrastructure.
